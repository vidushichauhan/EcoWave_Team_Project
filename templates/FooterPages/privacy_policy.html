Privacy Policy

At EcoWave, your privacy is our priority. This policy outlines how we handle your personal data and the steps we take to protect it while you explore and enjoy our eco-friendly platform.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as your name, email address, delivery address, payment details, and account preferences. We may also gather data through cookies and analytics tools to improve your browsing experience.

How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us process your orders, personalize your experience, and communicate updates about your account and our services. Rest assured, we do not share your data with third parties without your explicit consent.

Protecting Your Data

We take data security seriously. Our platform employs advanced encryption and secure servers to safeguard your personal information. Only authorized personnel have access to your data, ensuring its confidentiality.

Cookies and Tracking

Cookies are used to enhance your browsing experience by remembering your preferences and enabling personalized features. You can manage your cookie settings anytime through your browser's preferences.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. If you have any concerns or questions about how we handle your data, please reach out to us at support@ecowave.com.
